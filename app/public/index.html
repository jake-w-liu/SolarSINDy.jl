Severity is set by the CME's southward field (Bz), known with confidence only at L1 (~30–60 min upstream). This system nowcasts Dst; multi-day confident-severity lead requires CME models not yet included.

100% open source (MIT). Data: NASA OMNI (L1 solar wind), Kyoto WDC (Dst), USGS/INTERMAGNET (ground dB/dt). Forecasts are locked when issued and scored only after the target is observed. This is a research tool, not an operational authority — for official alerts see NOAA SWPC.
